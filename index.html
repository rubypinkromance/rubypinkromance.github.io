Ruby Pink

Erotic Romance

Ruby Pink lives in a small town in Washingon state, where she spends her days writing steamy paranormal romance. She loves telling stories about smart, talented women and the men who make them weak in the knees. All her books are at least partly based on real experiences, but she'll never tell which parts.

She's just released the fourth book in the Agent Reid series, and is currently working on a longer story about a woman who finds unexpected love on an alien planet.
